Pele on Wheels

Pele on Wheels - Robo Soccer

Pele on Wheels (DAY 1)
GAMEPLAY

  • The robots will go one on one in this football tournament.
  • Each match would have 2 halves of 4 minutes each.
  • The bots from both the teams must attack by making goals and at the same time defendtheir own goalpost.
  • The bot can kick or drag the ball to score goals.
  • The team which enters the D-area of their opponents half will be penalized.
  • The ball used would be a small hard plastic football.
  • In case the game ends up in a dead lock where the bots or the ball are stationary in the arena for more than 15 seconds the timer will be paused and the game will resume with both bots at their respective D-areas
  • Fixtures will be decided on the spot with a lucky draw using chits
  • The winners of each game would qualify to the next level.
  • Each level would have its own scoring i.e. no accumulation of scores of the previous levels.

PENALTIES
  • Entangling your opponent’s robot in the wires coming from your robot.
  • Head on collision with your opponent.
  • Entering the D area in your opponents half.
  • Pushing the ball out of arena.

SCORING
  • 50 points for every goal
  • -10 for every penalty.
  • In case of tie:
    1. At Qualifiers: the team with less penalties wins.
    2. At Finals: 2 minutes to score a golden goal.

ROBOT SPECIFICATIONS
  • The bot should fit in a box of dimensions 250mm X 250mm
  • The weight limit of the robot for this competition is 5 Kg + 200g
  • The voltage should not be more than 24V.
  • The bot can be wired or wireless. The wireless bot shouldn’t be connected with any otherdevice (example cell phones) except a unique controller.
  • The robots shall be inspected before the event. Failing the inspection shall lead to disqualification.
  • Pneumatic/Hydraulic/Cutter/Projectile/Entanglement etc. mechanisms are NOT allowed.
  • Damaging the arena in any way will lead to disqualification.

GENERAL RULES
  • Each team can have a maximum of 3 participants.
  • A team may consist of students from different colleges.
  • One person cannot be part of two teams.
  • Only one person from the team can control the bot at one point of time.
  • Maximum 2 members per team will be allowed to remain close to field for operating the Robots.
  • No damage should be made by a bot to the arena or to other team’s bots during the match any manner.
  • The organizers reserve the right to change the rules as they deem fit.
  • When a team is called for match, they must report within five minutes. Students must bring their college ID card.
  • Judges decision will be final.

MEXTRAPTION

SUPER CONTRAPTION (DAY 2)
MEXTRAPTION  is a deliberately complex contraption in which a series of devices that perform simple tasks are linked together to produce a domino effect in which activating one device triggers the next device in the sequence. The contestants are required to use the provided materials to initiate a continuous chain reaction so that the final output is obtained.
Event Rules

  • Registration fee: 50/ Head.

  • After setting up, the chain reaction will be initiated and the time taken for the final output is recorded.
  • Contestants are not allowed to interfere with the layout once the chain reaction is initiated.
  • Tools provided: Ruler, Vernier caliper, Calculator, Balls of varying diameters .Wooden Blocks.
  • Contestants are required to use only the materials provided.
