4'S FOOTBALL

FUN FOURS RULES

    LAW 1 THE NUMBER OF PLAYERS
  1. The maximum number of players in a team shall be 6, of whom 4 can be on the field of play at any given time. (4 outfield players and no goalkeeper)
  2. Players can be substituted at any point in the game.
  3. Players who have been substituted can be reintroduced to the game at a later stage (rolling substitutes)
    4. LAW 2 OFFSIDE
  4. There will be no offside in play.
    5. LAW 3 INFRINGEMENTS AND FOUL PLAY
  5. All free kicks awarded for infringements or foul play shall be direct.
    6. LAW 4 STARTING AND RESTARTING THE GAME
  6. The game shall be started with a centre ball.
  7. The game shall be restarted with a centre ball after a goal is scored.
  8. The game shall be restarted with a kick-in if the ball goes out of play.
  9. If the goal difference in any game reaches NINE, the game should stop.
  10. Each half will be of 7 minutes
    11. LAW 5 IF THE GOAL DIFFERENCE IS NIL
  11. A sudden death shoot-out from the centre.

AeroTrac

AEROTRAC (DAY 2)
Rules and Regulations:

  • Ready-made Quadcopter kits and use of any Automated Features of Flight Control Board (such as Position Hold, Altitude Hold, etc.) is strictly prohibited.
  • Number of motors used in the drone should not exceed 4 and size is restricted within 500 sized frames.
  • Maximum number of members in a team is 4.Only one member of each team is permitted in the flying zone.
  • Premature start before the official tone, pilot will lose 1 Lap.
  • The events consist of 2 rounds.
  • First round: 2 Laps provided to each team and best lap timing will be recorded.
  • Second round: Obstacle course.
  • Maximum of one fault is allowed which includes touching the ground, crossing maximum height limit and any collision with the arena or crossing the arena. Your second attempt would start at the fault spot. Time will not be paused and the second touchdown will be considered your final touchdown.
  • If the team thinks that their multi-copter is not functioning properly just after starting from the origin, the team can land the multi-copter before covering 5 meters in the path and will be given a second chance. No other requests for a second chance will be entertained.
  • Selection of teams for the second round will be based on their lap timing in first round.
  • The DRONE should be at a safe distance from the flyer as well as the public present near the arena.
  • Testing or flying DRONE out of the permissible region can lead to disqualification.
  • All participants must ensure safety precautions at all time.
  • No celebration laps or excessive displays of celebration while race is active.
  • Un-sportsman like conduct will not be tolerated.
  • Violation of any of the above rules or any misconduct will lead to DISQUALIFICATION. If there is any change in rules will be announced at the venue before the event begins and notified to the registered teams. The organization hold the power to disqualify or deregister the team without being answerable to anyone if any fraudulent behavior is noticed. If the event is cancelled the registration fees will be refunded to the respective teams.
  • Complimentary food coupons will be issued for all participants
  • If number of participants are below 15, registration fees and prize money will be reduced.
